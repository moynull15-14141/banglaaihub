## Why This Policy Exists

Bangla AI Hub's reputation depends on the quality and trustworthiness of its resources. A platform full of low-quality, unethical, or illegal datasets is worse than no platform at all — it poisons the ecosystem we are trying to build.

This policy protects:
- **Contributors** — clear rules so they know what to submit
- **Users** — trust that resources meet a standard
- **The community** — no harmful or illegal content
- **The platform** — legal and ethical standing

Every dataset submitted to Bangla AI Hub is evaluated against this policy.

---

## What We Accept

### ✅ Accepted Dataset Types

**Text Datasets**
- Bangla text corpora (news, literature, social media — properly sourced)
- Bangla sentiment analysis datasets
- Bangla named entity recognition (NER) datasets
- Bangla question-answering datasets
- Bangla machine translation pairs (Bangla ↔ English, Bangla ↔ Hindi, etc.)
- Bangla text classification datasets
- Bangla summarization datasets
- Bangla dialogue datasets

**Speech & Audio Datasets**
- Bangla speech recognition datasets
- Bangla text-to-speech (TTS) datasets
- Bangla dialect recordings (with speaker consent)
- Bangla keyword spotting datasets

**Visual & Multimodal Datasets**
- Bangla OCR datasets (handwritten and printed)
- Bangla sign language datasets
- Bangla image captioning datasets
- Documents/forms with Bangla text (with proper rights)

**Specialized Datasets**
- Bangla medical text datasets (de-identified)
- Bangla legal text datasets
- Bangla educational content datasets
- Bangla social media datasets (public posts, anonymized)
- Bangla code-switching datasets (Banglish)

---

## What We Reject

### ❌ Rejected — Always (No Exceptions)

**Privacy Violations**
- Datasets containing personally identifiable information (PII) without explicit consent
- Phone numbers, email addresses, national ID numbers, bank details
- Private messages, private social media posts, private conversations
- Medical records with patient identifiers
- Biometric data without explicit research consent

**Illegal Content**
- Datasets derived from copyrighted material without permission or fair use justification
- Content that violates Bangladesh's Digital Security Act or Indian IT laws
- Datasets containing illegal content of any kind

**Harmful Content**
- Datasets designed to train hate speech, harassment, or discrimination models
- Content targeting individuals for harm
- Deepfake training data for non-consensual use
- Misinformation or propaganda datasets

**Fabricated or Dishonest Data**
- Synthetic datasets presented as real human-generated data without disclosure
- Datasets with fabricated labels or annotations
- Plagiarized datasets (submitted under a different name than the original source)

## Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements for All Datasets

| Requirement | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Clear description** | What is this dataset, what is it for, how was it collected |
| **License specified** | Must have a clear license (CC0, CC-BY, MIT, custom) |
| **Size stated** | Number of examples, file size |
| **Language noted** | Bangla dialect, script (Unicode), language mix |
| **Collection method** | How was this data collected (web crawl, human annotation, survey, etc.) |
| **Contact person** | Someone responsible for questions about the dataset |

### Quality Tiers

**Tier 1 — Community Submitted**
- Passes basic requirements above
- Not independently verified
- Labeled "Community Submitted" on platform

**Tier 2 — Verified**
- Reviewed by at least 2 platform moderators
- Sample checked for quality and accuracy
- Labeled "Verified" with reviewer names

**Tier 3 — Certified**
- Full independent review by domain expert
- Published with quality report
- Labeled "Certified" — highest trust level

## Licensing Guide

### Licenses We Recommend

| License | What It Allows | Best For |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| **CC0 (Public Domain)** | Complete freedom, no restrictions | Maximum reuse |
| **CC-BY 4.0** | Use freely, must credit creator | Academic datasets |
| **CC-BY-SA 4.0** | Use freely, must credit, derivatives same license | Community datasets |
| **CC-BY-NC 4.0** | Non-commercial use only, must credit | Research-only datasets |
| **MIT** | Very permissive, credit required | Code-adjacent datasets |
| **Custom / Restricted** | Specified by creator | Special cases |

### What We Will Not Accept
- "All Rights Reserved" with no usage permissions
- Licenses that prohibit academic or research use
- Licenses that require payment for basic academic use

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Prepare Your Dataset
- Ensure it meets all minimum quality requirements
- Choose a clear license
- Write a description (minimum 200 words)
- Prepare sample files (minimum 100 examples for preview)

### Step 2: Submit
- Use the "Submit Dataset" form on the platform
- Fill all required fields
- Upload sample or link to full dataset
- Agree to the contributor terms

### Step 3: Review
- Platform team reviews within 7 working days
- Community Submitted: basic check (2-3 days)
- Verified: full review (5-7 days)
- You will receive feedback via email

### Step 4: Publication
- Approved datasets are published publicly
- You receive contributor credit
- Dataset is indexed and searchable

## Contributor Responsibilities

By submitting a dataset to Bangla AI Hub, you confirm:

1. You have the right to share this data
2. You have disclosed all information about how it was collected
3. You have not included any PII without proper consent
4. The license you selected accurately reflects what users may do with the data
5. You will respond to questions or corrections within 30 days
6. If a problem is found, you will cooperate with the review team

---

## Dataset Removal Policy

A published dataset may be removed if:

- The original contributor requests removal
- A valid copyright claim is received
- Privacy violation is discovered
- The dataset is found to contain harmful content
- The dataset is found to be fabricated or mislabeled

Removed datasets are marked as "Withdrawn" with an explanation. Files are deleted within 30 days of removal decision.

---

## Appeals Process

If your dataset is rejected and you believe the decision is wrong:

1. Contact the review team with your case
2. Provide additional documentation or clarification
3. The team will re-review within 14 days
4. Final decision is communicated with explanation

We aim to be fair. Rejection is not permanent if the issue can be fixed.

## For Different User Types

### As a Developer
You want to use datasets for training or testing models. Check the license carefully — CC0 and CC-BY allow commercial use, CC-BY-NC does not. Always cite the dataset in your work.

### As a Researcher / Academic
You can use most datasets for research purposes under academic fair use. For publication, ensure you have the rights to use the data. Prefer Verified or Certified tier datasets for serious research.

### As a Student
Free to use any dataset for learning and personal projects. For course projects or thesis work, check with your supervisor whether the license allows your use case.

### As a Business / Organization
Review licenses carefully. CC-BY-NC datasets cannot be used for commercial products. For commercial applications, look for CC0, CC-BY, or MIT licensed datasets. Contact the contributor if unsure.

### As a Government or Public Institution
You may use datasets for public service purposes. Contact us if you need special licensing arrangements for large-scale public projects.
