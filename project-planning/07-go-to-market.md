## GTM Philosophy

Our go-to-market is not a launch campaign. It is a slow, deliberate process of earning trust with a specific community over a long period of time.

We are not trying to go viral. We are trying to become indispensable.

The Bangla AI community is small and well-networked. Word travels fast. If we serve the first 100 people exceptionally well, they will tell the next 1,000. If we ship a mediocre product, no amount of marketing will fix it.

**Principle: Earn distribution before seeking reach.**

---

## Target Audience Priority

We do not market to everyone. We market to specific people in a specific sequence.

### Tier 1 (Launch Audience — 0 to 500 users)
**Who:** People already doing Bangla AI work

Characteristics:
- Already have GitHub accounts
- Already contribute to HuggingFace or research papers
- Already in Bangla AI conversations on Twitter/X and LinkedIn
- Frustrated by the fragmented state of Bangla AI resources
- Will recognize the value of the platform immediately

Why first: They don't need convincing. They already have the pain we solve.

### Tier 2 (Growth Audience — 500 to 5,000 users)
**Who:** Students and developers curious about AI in the Bangla context

Characteristics:
- Computer science, EEE, or related degree students in Bangladesh
- Junior developers wanting to contribute to open source
- People who speak Bangla and are interested in AI
- Looking for a community and learning resources

Why second: They need the social proof and content that Tier 1 creates.

### Tier 3 (Scale Audience — 5,000+ users)
**Who:** Researchers, academics, and professionals

Characteristics:
- University professors and research labs
- AI professionals at companies
- International researchers working on South Asian languages
- Policy makers and government organizations

Why third: They are high-value but require institutional credibility first.

## Pre-Launch Strategy (Q1-Q2 2026)

### Build in Public
Document the building process openly:
- Regular updates on Twitter/X and LinkedIn about what is being built
- Share design decisions, technical challenges, and community philosophy
- Post early mockups, prototypes, and feedback
- Engage the community in decisions ("Should the platform support this feature?")

**Why this works:** Creates anticipation, builds followers before launch, and signals authenticity.

### Content Before Product
Before the platform launches, create and distribute content that demonstrates expertise:
- "The State of Bangla AI: What Exists and What's Missing" (comprehensive post)
- "Why Training Data is the Biggest Problem for Bangla Language Models" (educational)
- "A Curated List of Every Bangla NLP Dataset on HuggingFace" (practical value)
- "Bangla Tokenization Explained" (technical, shareable)

These pieces establish credibility and capture search traffic even before the platform exists.

### The Founding 50 Program
Personally identify and invite 50 people who are already active in Bangla AI:
- Direct outreach via Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and email
- Invite them to shape the platform before public launch
- Give them early access, Founder badges, and direct influence on features
- Their early endorsement is the most credible marketing possible

## Launch Strategy (Q3-Q4 2026)

### Private Beta (Q3 2026)
- 50-200 hand-selected users
- Intensive feedback collection
- Rapid iteration based on real usage
- Build testimonials and success stories
- Let early users co-create community standards

**Criteria for public launch readiness:**
- Core search works reliably
- 200+ curated resources indexed
- Community forum is active
- No critical bugs in core flows
- At least 20 users have successfully published resources

### Public Launch (Q4 2026)

**Platform Channels:**

1. **Product Hunt**
   - Schedule for a Tuesday/Wednesday
   - Build hunter list (people who will upvote on Day 1)
   - Prepare launch post, screenshots, and product video
   - Engage comments throughout the day
   - Target: Top 5 of the day

2. **Hacker News**
   - Post "Show HN: Bangla AI Hub — Open Ecosystem for Bangla AI"
   - Genuine, non-spammy launch post
   - Founder personally engages every comment

3. **Twitter/X**
   - Launch thread with the story, mission, and product
   - Tag key people in the Bangla AI and NLP community
   - Post screenshots and feature highlights

4. **LinkedIn**
   - Professional post targeting Bangladeshi tech community
   - Reach university professors and researchers
   - Share in relevant groups (Bangladesh Tech, AI/ML groups)

5. **Facebook Groups**
   - Bangladeshi developer Facebook groups (major reach in Bangladesh)
   - University CS groups
   - AI and tech communities

6. **Reddit**
   - r/MachineLearning (if relevant framing)
   - r/LanguageTechnology
   - r/bangladesh

7. **HuggingFace**
   - Post in HuggingFace community
   - Tag when indexing HuggingFace datasets
   - Cross-link between HuggingFace and Bangla AI Hub

**PR & Media:**
- Reach out to Bangladesh tech media (Prothom Alo Tech, The Daily Star Tech)
- Pitch to international AI newsletters (The Sequence, Import AI)
- Connect with AI conferences that cover low-resource languages

## Post-Launch Distribution Channels

### Organic Search (SEO)
This is our most important long-term channel.

**Why:** When someone searches "Bangla NLP dataset", "Bengali speech recognition data", or "Bangla sentiment analysis", we want to appear first.

**Strategy:**
- Every resource page is indexed and SEO-optimized
- Resource titles and descriptions are crafted for discoverability
- Category pages target specific long-tail keywords
- Blog content targets "how to" and "what is" queries in Bangla AI
- Build backlinks through partnerships, press, and high-quality content

**Target Keywords:**
- "Bangla AI dataset"
- "Bengali NLP resources"
- "Bangla speech corpus"
- "Bangla language model"
- "Bangladesh AI research"
- "Bangla NER dataset"
- "Bengali text classification"

### Community-Driven Distribution
The most cost-effective channel. When our users share our resources, we reach people we never could reach through advertising.

**Mechanisms:**
- Every resource page is easily shareable (clean URL, good meta description, Open Graph tags)
- "Cite this dataset" button for researchers
- "Share this resource" one-click sharing
- Community contribution credits encourage sharing
- GitHub integration means resources appear in GitHub ecosystems

### University Partnerships
Universities are distribution multipliers.

**Strategy:**
- Partner with CS department heads at top universities in Bangladesh and West Bengal
- Offer to host university research and datasets on the platform
- Create university leaderboard for contributions
- Speak at university AI events and symposiums
- Sponsor student AI hackathons

**Target Universities (Year 1):**
- BUET (Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology)
- Dhaka University
- BRAC University
- North South University
- Jahangirnagar University
- IIT Kharagpur (West Bengal)
- Jadavpur University (West Bengal)

### Creator & Influencer Distribution
Bangla tech community has active voices on YouTube and LinkedIn.

**Strategy:**
- Identify 20-30 active Bangla tech content creators
- Offer platform access, featured placement, and contributor status
- Encourage them to share resources they discover
- Do not pay for promotion — earn it through genuine value

### HuggingFace & GitHub Integration
The Bangla AI developer community is on HuggingFace and GitHub.

**Strategy:**
- Mirror/index all Bangla datasets from HuggingFace with added metadata
- Create GitHub organization for Bangla AI Hub
- Contribute to open-source Bangla AI projects
- Build GitHub Action that can auto-submit datasets to Bangla AI Hub
- Get listed in awesome-bangla-nlp and similar curated lists

## What We Will NOT Do

**No paid advertising in Year 1**
We cannot buy our way into the community's trust. Every dollar spent on ads is a dollar not spent on building.

**No engagement farming**
No clickbait titles, no controversial takes for reach, no manufactured drama. Every piece of content must be genuinely useful.

**No vanity partnerships**
We will not sign MOUs with organizations that don't actively contribute. Partnerships must have real deliverables.

**No follower-buying or fake engagement**
Our community metrics will be real. A platform of 100 deeply engaged users is worth more than 10,000 inactive accounts.
