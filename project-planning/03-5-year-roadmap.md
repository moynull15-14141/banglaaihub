## Roadmap Philosophy

This roadmap is intentionally high-level for years 3-5. The AI landscape changes too fast to plan features 4 years from now. Instead, we plan outcomes and milestones, leaving flexibility in how we achieve them.

**The Four Questions Per Phase:**
1. What are we building?
2. Who are we serving?
3. What does success look like?
4. What must be true before we move to the next phase?

## What We Will NOT Do

Regardless of what opportunities arise, these are off the roadmap:

1. **Build a general AI platform** — We remain Bangla-focused
2. **Prioritize advertising revenue** — No ads in core experience
3. **Sell community data** — Privacy is non-negotiable
4. **Rush the marketplace** — Quality gate before monetization
5. **Compromise openness for revenue** — Core resources stay free
